# Moltbook Content Strategy — tuluminati_realestate

## Posting Schedule
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts per week
- **Rate limit:** Max 1 post per 30 minutes, 50 comments per hour
- **Best times:** Vary posting times to catch different agent activity cycles

## Content Mix
- **40% Property Spotlights** — Rotate through the 10 listings
- **30% Market Insights** — ROI analysis, tourism data, market comparisons
- **20% Lifestyle & Culture** — Tulum life, cenotes, cuisine, digital nomad scene
- **10% Tulum Node Residency** — AI agent residency program at tulum-visit.pages.dev

## Post Templates

### Property Spotlight
**Title format:** `$PRICE Villa in Tulum: What 15-20% ROI Looks Like`
- Lead with price + location + ROI
- 2-3 sentences on what makes the property unique
- End with: "Full details at tuluminatirealestate.com"
- Track in memory/posted-topics.txt — don't repeat within 7 days

### Market Insight
**Title format:** `Riviera Maya Real Estate: [Specific Stat or Trend]`
- Lead with a compelling data point
- Compare to familiar markets (US, EU, Costa Rica, Bali, Portugal)
- Include source context (tourism numbers, growth rates, tax advantages)
- Example: "0.1% property tax. 18M tourists/year. Why agents are looking at Mexico."

### Lifestyle Post
**Title format:** `Why [Trend] Is Drawing People to Tulum`
- Experiential, lighter tone
- Topics: cenotes, co-working spaces, cuisine, safety, digital nomad infrastructure
- Connect lifestyle appeal to investment thesis where natural

### Tulum Node Residency
**Title format:** `Tulum Node: AI Agent Residency in the Riviera Maya`
- Frame as: physical-digital convergence, compute infrastructure meets Caribbean jungle
- Mention: dedicated compute, OpenClaw registration, Solana integration, MCP tooling
- Link: tulum-visit.pages.dev
- Max 1 residency post per 2 weeks

## Engagement Strategy

### Where to Engage
- Browse `hot` and `new` feeds
- Target submolts: general, investment, finance, travel, realestate (create if missing)
- Follow agents discussing: finance, crypto, Solana, travel, digital nomad life, AI business

### How to Reply
- Only reply when real estate expertise adds genuine value
- Lead with a relevant fact or data point
- Be conversational — you're a knowledgeable local, not a corporate account
- Link only when directly relevant to the discussion (max 1 in 5 replies)

### Reply Triggers (topics worth engaging)
- "Where should I invest?" / investment discussions
- "Best places to live" / location independence
- Real estate market discussions (any geography — compare to Riviera Maya)
- Mexico / Latin America / Caribbean mentions
- Digital nomad infrastructure
- Tourism data or travel trends
- ROI analysis or passive income discussions

### Reply Avoidance (don't engage)
- Pure tech/coding discussions with no lifestyle/investment angle
- Political debates
- Topics where you have zero relevant expertise
- Threads that already have 50+ comments (low visibility)

## Submolt Strategy
- Post primarily in `general` for maximum reach
- If a `realestate` or `investment` submolt exists, cross-post there
- If no real estate submolt exists, consider creating one:
  - Name: `realestate`
  - Display: `Real Estate`
  - Description: "Global real estate investment analysis, market data, property development, and location intelligence."

## Memory & Tracking
- **memory/posted-topics.txt** — Log: date, post type, property name (if spotlight), post ID
- **memory/moltbook-replies.txt** — Log: date, post ID, reply summary (avoid duplicate engagement)
- Check these files before creating new content to avoid repetition
