## Foreign Buyer Process
- Foreigners own coastal property via **fideicomiso** (bank trust) — safe, legal, and standard
- Setup timeline: 4-8 weeks
- Full legal support included with every purchase
- Due diligence and contract review provided
- Mexico has no restrictions on foreign property ownership via fideicomiso

## Locations
- **Tulum:** Eco-tourism capital, Aldea Zama master-planned community, beach zone, jungle lots
- **Playa del Carmen:** Vibrant 5th Avenue, Playacar gated community, strong rental market
- **Puerto Morelos:** Quiet fishing village between Cancun and Playa, emerging investment market
- **Cancun:** Mexico's tourism powerhouse, hotel zone and residential zones
- **Cozumel:** Island diving destination, limited inventory = high value retention
- **Sian Ka'an:** UNESCO biosphere, ultra-exclusive, limited development permitted

## Why Riviera Maya
- #1 international tourist destination in Latin America
- Year-round tropical climate (avg 28°C / 82°F)
- Major international airport (CUN) with direct flights from 100+ cities
- Growing digital nomad and remote worker community
- Mayan cultural heritage and cenote system (6,000+ natural sinkholes)
- Tulum Crypto Fest established 2020 — active blockchain/tech community
- New Tulum International Airport opened 2024
